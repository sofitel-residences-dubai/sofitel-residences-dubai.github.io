Payment Plan for Sofitel Residences Dubai

At Sofitel Residences Dubai, we understand the importance of providing a seamless and flexible payment plan to make your dream of owning a luxury home in the heart of Downtown Dubai a reality. Our payment plan is designed to offer you ease and convenience, ensuring a smooth transition to your new home.

To secure your residence at Sofitel, a 10% booking deposit is required. This initial payment locks in your chosen apartment, allowing you to start your journey towards owning a piece of luxury in one of Dubai's most prestigious locations.

Following your booking deposit, the payment plan is structured to accommodate your financial comfort. You will make a 10% payment upon signing the Sale and Purchase Agreement. This step is crucial as it formalizes your commitment to the property and initiates the construction process.

As construction progresses, you will make additional payments in a series of installments, totaling 70% of the property’s value. These payments are aligned with the construction milestones, ensuring that you only pay for the completed phases of the development. This phased approach provides you with peace of mind, knowing that your investment is secure and tied to the progress of the project.

Upon the completion of construction, a 20% final payment is due. This final installment is due upon handover, ensuring that you can take possession of your new residence without any outstanding balances. The handover process is designed to be seamless, with our dedicated team assisting you every step of the way to ensure a smooth transition into your new home.

Our payment plan is designed not only to be flexible but also to give you the peace of mind you deserve. With clear milestones and a well-structured timeline, you can rest assured that your investment in Sofitel Residences Dubai is both secure and rewarding.
